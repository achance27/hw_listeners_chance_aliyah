Weird Laws

Hello and welcome to a webpage of weird laws that are most likely not enforced unless absolutely necessary. Needless to say, do not try this at home. Hope you enjoy!

Florida

  • The state constitution allows for freedom of speech, a trial by jury, and pregnant pigs to not be confined in cages.
  • It is illegal to sing in a public place while attired in a swimsuit.
  • It is illegal to skateboard without a license.

California

  • In San Jose and Sunnyvale it is illegal for grocery stores to provide plastic bags.
  • You may only throw a frisbee at the beach in Los Angeles County with the lifeguard's permission.

Michigan

  • Cars may not be sold on Sunday.
  • Persons may not be drunk on trains.
